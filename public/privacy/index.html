Privacy Policy

Perigee collects contact, property, service, and appointment details only to respond to service requests, manage appointments, and operate the customer portal.

Information collected

When you submit an appointment request, Perigee may collect your name, email, phone number, property address, requested service, preferred appointment date/time, membership status, and notes you provide.

How it is used

This information is used to schedule service, contact you about requests, manage appointments, and provide property management services.
